About Me

My journey in academia and my passion for teaching are deeply rooted in the fascinating realms of Probability Theory, Mathematical Statistics, Data Structures, and Algorithms.

With a Master's Degree in Computer Science, which I proudly earned in 2022, I bring a strong foundation in these fields. I am here to guide you through the intricacies of probability, statistics, and the fundamental principles underlying data structures and algorithms. Whether you're exploring complex probability distributions or diving deep into efficient data manipulation, I am committed to helping you succeed.

My academic journey started with a Bachelor's Degree in Mathematics, which I completed in 2020. The love for mathematical concepts and their application in computer science has been a driving force in my educational pursuits.

Beyond the classroom, I am enthusiastic about assisting aspiring statisticians and computer scientists in preparing for their academic challenges and future careers. If you have questions, seek guidance, or simply want to explore the exciting world of data and computation, please don't hesitate to reach out. Let's embark on this learning journey together!
