About Me

Hello, I'm a dedicated teaching assistant specializing in the fascinating realms of Probability Theory and Mathematical Statistics, as well as Data Structures and Algorithms. I'm passionate about these subjects and thrive on sharing my knowledge and insights with students.

With a strong foundation in these fields, I'm here to help you unravel the intricacies of probability, statistics, and the fundamental principles that underlie data structures and algorithms. Whether you're grappling with complex probability distributions or diving deep into efficient data manipulation, I'm here to guide you every step of the way.

My commitment extends beyond the classroom. I'm eager to assist budding statisticians and computer scientists in preparing for their academic challenges and future careers. If you have questions, seek guidance, or simply want to explore the exciting world of data and computation, please don't hesitate to reach out.
